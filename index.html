Our Story

How It Started

2021 — FOUNDED
The Beginning

Three engineers frustrated by high brokerage fees quit their jobs and built QuantVest from a garage in San Francisco.

2022 — GROWTH
Series A

Raised $28M Series A, launched crypto trading, and grew to 200,000 users across 40 countries in under 12 months.

2023 — SCALE
Global Expansion

Expanded to 150 countries, launched stocks & ETF trading, hit $1B in total volume traded milestone.

2024–NOW
AI & Beyond

Launched AI-powered signals and copy trading. Now serving 2.4M investors with $6B+ in annual trading volume.
